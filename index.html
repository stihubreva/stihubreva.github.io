BACKGROUND

The Department of Science and Technology (DST) sponsored, Science Technology and Innovation hub at Chickaballapura, Karnataka is a funded project given to REVA University for the duration of 4 years from 2022-25. The project strives to promote inclusive growth, support environmental stewardship, and drive technological progress to build resilient and equitable communities where every individual can thrive.
